* [qProbConc](https://github.com/vegf17/qProbConc)

**qProbConc** is a Haskell interpreter for a concurrent, probabilistic, and quantum programming language.

The project is connected to my research on the semantics of programming languages combining concurrency with probabilistic and quantum effects.
