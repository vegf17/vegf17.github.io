### About me
* I am a PhD student at the University of Minho. My research focuses on the semantics of programming
  languages with concurrent, probabilistic, and/or quantum features. I enjoy implementing these
  languages in Haskell to ease the reasoning about programs. The languages I study have an
  imperative flavor, with a syntax similar to C, which I taught as an invited professor at the
  University of Minho

* Got a MSc degree in Physics Engineering (specialization in Physics of Information)

* Currently, I am starting to deepen my knoweledge in Python and in several DSL for quantum (e.g.:
  [Qiskit](https://www.ibm.com/quantum/qiskit),
  [PennyLane](https://pennylane.ai/),
  [Cirq](https://quantumai.google/cirq),
  [Qrisp](https://qrisp.eu/index.html)).

  Most of the things I do to learn can be consulted in the [playground of my GitHub](https://github.com/vegf17/playground)

### Research Area
* Semantics of concurrent programming languages mixed with probabilistic and/or quantum effects

* Event structures as a denotational model for concurrent languages

* Interpreters in Haskell for simple programming languages
